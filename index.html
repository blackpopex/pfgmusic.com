Welcome to my homepage

Man and music have a very strong relationship that has existed long before development and industrialization - the new age. Music is found in every culture and tradition of man, even in the most remote locations, civilization, traditions and cultures that are long gone and forgotten. Music over the years has played a fundamental role in education, culture, religion, creating awareness, business, relaxation; the list goes on and on because the application of music is quite endless especially in this current times.

music, however creates an atmosphere that enables us connect to God, an atmosphere that enables the move of God in ways that cannot be imagined. Christians all over the world have a strong attachment to music; in fact, most Christian gatherings will not be complete without a form of music. [...] The role and importance of music in the life of every believer cannot be over emphasized because almost every aspect of our meetings and life in general is characterized by one form of music or the other.

I like to believe that all humans are musical beings, we are all musical, we wake with it and sleep with it, we relax with it and work with it, we learn with it and teach with it, we sell and buy it; so music is essential to our optimal function as humans. Gospel music is composed and performed for many purposes, including aesthetic pleasure, religious or ceremonial purposes, and as an entertainment product for the marketplace. Gospel music usually has dominant vocals (often with strong use of harmony) with Christian lyrics. Gospel music can be traced to the early 17th century with roots in the black oral tradition.

At Perfect George Music, we understand the energy, strength and power that is embedded in music. We understand the negative implication that comes from constantly listening to music that has lyrics embedded with negative words; music with explicit content, violent or hate words. Over the years, we have been able to study the behavioral patterns in our society based on the kind of lyrical content that they have been exposed to and we have discovered that most people that constantly listen to lyrics with strong sexual undertone, drug abuse approval, violent and hate words tend to think in such manner and as such begin to act or behave in such a way. Most of them end up becoming addicted to living these lifestyles that they have constantly heard or listened to.

Based on this understanding, we began to create lyrical content that is deliberately designed to pass the right information to our society because it is actually impossible to take music out from our lives. Perfect George Music is committed to creating music for a specific target audience and that is the Young People in the streets and the Young At Heart, people who may not have any form of attraction for the kind of music played in the churches. Our ministry is designed to create music that present the gospel in such a way that it will attract them.

This is why our songs are usually heavily worded and very vibrant, full of energy and power, loaded with positive words that have the ability to transform any mind that pays attention to its content. We choose music as a form of therapy for our society because we understand the level of power and influence it carries.
